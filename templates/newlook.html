All About Summing Up

This software helps you in FOUR different aspects. First, it helps you in SUMMARIZING any article in 5-6 lines providing with the same meaning in brief. Secondly, it provides you with a VOICE CONVERTOR which shorten your typing work and you may also listen to your text, and thirdly you can provide text in case there is noise problems in your area and last you can provide the text which is saved in your laptop/desktop to get it's summary
